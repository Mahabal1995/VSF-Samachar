1. Sri Krishna Janmashtami
Do You ❤️ Krishna?

This Janmashtami, celebrate Lord Krishna with 11-day days of chanting and Abhishekam.

Immerse yourselves in Sri Krishna’s devotion and love. Save the Dates: 6th - 16th September 2023

Express your love for Krishna by doing:

1. Maha Mantra Japa in morning - 11 mins
2. Abhishekam with Devotional Chant in the evening - 7 mins

Conclude your worship with a collective Yagya (Medium, 10 min) performed by thousands of devotess across the globe on 16th Sept '23.

If you wish to:

1. Unlock 4 Puranic stories on September 6th, 9th, 13th, and 16th, by completing rituals and learn their esoteric meaning.
2. Participate in 2 live meditation sessions on September 8th and 15th.

Then, this is Perfect for You!
